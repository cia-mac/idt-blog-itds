The XSLink Hub aggregates two USB-C camera streams into a single QSFP fiber connection. This allows compact cameras like the XSM (200g) and OS II (400g) to connect to rack-mount processors over long fiber runs. Each hub occupies one fiber port on the processor. An RT IV with 2 fiber ports supports 2 hubs, giving access to 4 USB-C cameras. An RT V with 4 ports supports up to 8 cameras through 4 hubs. Cameras connected through a hub run at half the maximum frame rate of a direct connection.
