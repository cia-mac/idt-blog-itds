Phoenix GOLD (Offboard)

Resolution: 5120 x 2448
Frame rate: 2,000 fps
FOV: Lens-dependent (wide lenses typical)
Weight: 1,000g
Mount: Canon EF, tripod or overhead rig
Purpose: Full vehicle trajectory, barrier contact geometry, overall deformation envelope

Design principle: Onboard cameras capture what offboard cameras cannot see: the view from inside the crumple zone, behind the airbag, under the dashboard. Offboard cameras provide spatial context and measurement coverage across the full vehicle width. A complete crash test setup uses both types in overlapping positions to eliminate blind spots. FOV values shown in this diagram are illustrative. Actual field of view depends on the lens mounted to each camera.
